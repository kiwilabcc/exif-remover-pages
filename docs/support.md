# Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does Privacy Eraser work?

Privacy Eraser scans your photos for hidden EXIF metadata — GPS coordinates, camera details, timestamps, and other identifying information — and creates cleaned copies with that metadata removed. Your original photos are never modified.

### Is my data safe?

Yes. All photo scanning and metadata cleaning happens entirely on your device. Your photos and their metadata are never uploaded, transmitted, or sent to any server. No data leaves your phone.

### Does the app modify my original photos?

No. Privacy Eraser creates new copies of your photos with the metadata removed. Your originals remain untouched in your photo library.

### What EXIF data is removed?

The app removes common EXIF fields including GPS coordinates, camera model and manufacturer, timestamps, software information, and other metadata tags embedded in your image files.

### Why would I want to remove EXIF data?

Photos you share online often contain hidden metadata that can reveal your location, the device you used, and when the photo was taken. Removing this metadata protects your privacy before sharing.

### Is the app free?

Yes, Privacy Eraser is free to use.
