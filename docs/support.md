# Support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does Photo Privacy: EXIF Eraser work?

Photo Privacy: EXIF Eraser scans your photos for hidden EXIF metadata — GPS coordinates, camera details, timestamps, and other identifying information — and creates cleaned copies on supported processing paths. Your original photos are never modified.

### Is my data safe?

Yes. All photo scanning and metadata cleaning happens entirely on your device. Your photos and their metadata are never uploaded, transmitted, or sent to any server. No data leaves your phone.

### Does the app modify my original photos?

No. Photo Privacy: EXIF Eraser creates new copies of your photos with metadata removed from the processed copy. Your originals remain untouched in your photo library.

### What EXIF data is removed?

The app targets common EXIF metadata such as GPS coordinates, camera details, timestamps, software information, and other metadata present in the image file passed into the app. Exact fields can vary by image and processing path.

### Why would I want to remove EXIF data?

Photos you share online often contain hidden metadata that can reveal your location, the device you used, and when the photo was taken. Removing this metadata protects your privacy before sharing.

### Is the app free?

Yes, Photo Privacy: EXIF Eraser is free to use.
